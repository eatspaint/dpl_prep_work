Symbols, Arrays & Hashes

This lesson will begin by reviewing all of the parts of Ruby you should know by now. After the review, we will move on to learn a little more about arrays, and finally one of the more challenging concepts for beginners: Hashes.

After this section, you should:
  • Feel more comfortable with Ruby’s symbols and key words
  • Know how to use and manipulate arrays to a better degree
  • Know what a hash is in Ruby
  • Know how to set up and manipulate a hash
  • Know how to access data from a hash
